HIRING PROCESS

Screening

Your application gets reviewed by us. Let’s have a 20 minutes talk via phone or skype. Then your profile goes to the hiring team.

1st Interview

Meet a hiring manager and get the feedback within 1 week.

Challenge

Once you get a real world practical task, the company is seriously considering working with you. So it’s worth your time and effort!

2nd Interview

The challenge feedback is positive, the 2nd interview will follow. Meet with other potential future teammates and a peer from another team and probably HR who you would closely work with..

Offer stage

The last step of the recruitment process. If you convinced the team and they convinced you, they will prepare an offer for you. Please, be patient, this stage may take some time.
